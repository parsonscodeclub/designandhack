How to sponsor

Design & Hack is the first student hackathon focused on design.

Design & Hack will take place over the weekend of April 18-20 in NYC. We are expecting 400 student designers and developers from top schools across the country. They will collaborate to create projects that look great and work beautifully.

This hackathon is set apart from others in two ways:

  1. The attendees: Designers who understand technology and are interested in coding, and Developers who are product-minded and interested in design.
  2. Final projects that are polished both in aesthetics and in flow, and more likely to continue beyond the hackathon.

“Hackathons would be 10x better if every team had a designer.”

—Dave Fontenot, M-Hacks Founder
